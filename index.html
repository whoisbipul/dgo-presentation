The Insight: If a user gets a "GOAL!" notification on their phone before they see it on the stream, they feel cheated. We target < 6s latency.

The "Motion" Standard

25fps vs. 50fps (Frame Rate)

25 FPS Nepal Standard Broadcast
50/60 FPS Global Sports Standard

Why it matters: In movies, 24fps is "cinematic." In sports, fast-moving objects (cricket balls) disappear or "ghost" at low frame rates. DGO is the first in Nepal to enforce a strict 50fps pipeline.
