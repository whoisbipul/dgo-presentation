The "Motion" Standard

25fps vs. 50fps (Frame Rate)

25 FPS Nepal Standard Broadcast
50/60 FPS Global Sports Standard

Why it matters: In movies, 24fps is "cinematic." In sports, fast-moving objects (cricket balls) disappear or "ghost" at low frame rates. DGO is the first in Nepal to enforce a strict 50fps pipeline.

A followable sports “show” that keeps people coming back — even when there’s no match.

Why Story Selling beats Match Selling on OTT

  • OTT is a relationship: we can build a habit (episodes + returns), not just a match spike.
  • Data makes it measurable: minutes watched, completion, return frequency, and conversions — not “guesswork reach.”
  • Interactivity converts attention: votes, predictions, offers, and login-gated BTS turn viewers into actions.
  • Better for low sports literacy: stories explain “who/why” so casual audiences stay longer.
  • More sellable inventory: every episode creates premium brand moments (not only matchday slots).

What Works in Nepal (Fast, Low-Cost Formats)

🏏
Cricket
Heroes + rivalries + simple explainers convert casual viewers.
  • Hero journey: from local nets → national spotlight.
  • Rivalry week: two teams, two cultures, one narrative.
  • Explainers: “what changed this over” in simple language.
  • Mic’d up practice: the human side before matchday.
⚽
Football
Identity stories: pride, grit, and “what’s at stake”.
  • Dressing room: team talk + emotions before kickoff.
  • Rivalry + identity: “who we are” as a club/community.
  • Set-piece stories: the plan behind the moment.
  • Fan POV: travel + watch party + reaction shorts.
🏐
Volleyball
Underdog arcs + team culture — fast, high-emotion episodes.
  • Bootcamp life: training grind in short episodes.
  • Clutch moments: “one rally that flips the set.”
  • Team chemistry: roles, rituals, and pressure.
  • Beginner explainers: rotations & rules in 60 seconds.
🎮
Esports
Personalities + clips + community challenges drive daily returns.
  • Road to qualifiers: team grind + scrim diaries.
  • IGL / coach cam: how decisions are made mid-fight.
  • Meta explainers: simple breakdown of “why this strategy.”
  • Player lifestyle: routines, setups, and mindset.
