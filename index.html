1. The Ecosystem: Rights & IP

We don't "make" the game; we buy the right to show it. This is how the inventory works.

What are "Rights"?

A license from a governing body (e.g., ICC, FIFA, League Boards) permitting us to transmit the live match within a specific territory.

  • Exclusive: Only we show it (High Value).
  • Non-Exclusive: Shared with others (Low Cost).
  • Digital vs. Linear: App vs. TV rights.

Our Target Assets

Tier 1: High Impact
Major Cricket Leagues & Top Football. Drivers of subscription acquisition.
Tier 2: Retention Content
B-Leagues, Highlights, Classic Matches. Keeps users engaged daily.
